why choose us?

At Ji'L Academy, our commitment lies in RECOGNIZING, CULTIVATING, ENHANCING, and GALVANIZING the unique gifts within YOUR CHILD for the glory of God. We believe that education should be an adventure rather than just a collection of lessons. Here, students experience an ideal balance of excitement and learning, with engaging academic challenges paired with exhilarating activities, creating unforgettable experiences along the way.

popular courses

At Ji’L Academy of Beyond Excellence, we provide seven (7) Educational Options for Life-Long Learners:

1. Face-2-Face Learners: Our school is open from 8:00 AM to 12:00 NOON, Monday through Thursday. Please ensure that your scholar arrives on time to start the day. Our Afterschool Program offers educational electives, tutoring, and enrichment activities Monday – Thursday from 12:00 Noon to 5:00 PM and Friday School from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

2. Synchronous Learners: Meeting times are scheduled for specific assignments, including Bible Wisdom and Leadership at 8:00 AM, as well as Project Presentations, Tests, and Exams.

3. Dual Advanced Learners: Our meeting times are flexible by semester for college studies. In-person attendance for School projects and tests is assigned by availability.

4. Asynchronous Learners: The scholar is independent, and the work is turned in by the due date. Tests are online and timed for a more flexible learning environment.

5. Elevation Learners: Independent course study with required College studies for an extended academic increase.

6. Exceptional Learners: Scholars who are finishing state requirements standards with college studies. Meeting time is based on the Scholar's complete requirements for the state of Louisiana.

7. Collegiate Learners: Scholars who are only enrolled in college courses study with tutoring assistance.Tutoring hours are on demand.

There are six (6) G.R.O.W.T.H. steps for Ji’L Academy of Beyond Excellence Enrollment

1. GATHER for Parent and Student Consultation

2. REPORT on student information for the Application Process

3. ONLINE interview and Letters of Recommendation for Final Review

4. WALK-THROUGH the Ji’L Academy School Handbook

5. TERMS are Understood and Agreed

6. HONORED with an Acceptance Letter
